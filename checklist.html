Checklist

Note_1: hardware development starts only in step four, the preparation phase is important if one does not want to end up reinventing the wheel of producing useless hardware. Note_2: Step 1-3 have an iterative nature: Especially, the analysis of "Similar projects" coming in step 3, and the prototyping (step 4) are often giving new ideas and refining use cases.
